Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 19, 2026


  • This website never asks for your Google, Microsoft, Kodi, or email password. Authentication is performed only through the official provider authorization pages.
  • The add-on stores only your account name, account ID, and access data locally on your Kodi device only. This is used to display your account as an option in Kodi and to access your drive files.
  • The add-on accesses your file information only from your Kodi device directly to the cloud drive provider.
  • The add-on uses your file information only to display it in Kodi so you can play videos, play music, or view pictures.
  • The add-on transmits or shares diagnostic information with the developer only if you explicitly agree to it and only when an error happens, for the sole purpose of helping resolve issues.
    This information may include technical diagnostic data such as file identifiers, filenames, error messages, and anonymous connection metadata.
    Sensitive information such as passwords, access tokens, email addresses, usernames, or personal file contents are never collected.
    You can always disable this option in the configuration menu.
  • Each permission scope requested by the add-on has a specific purpose:

    OneDrive:
    Scope Use/Purpose
    files.read.all Access your file information in read-only mode to navigate your files and play them in Kodi.
    sites.read.all Access your site files for business accounts in read-only mode to navigate your files and play them in Kodi.
    profile Access your profile information to display your account as an option in Kodi.

    Google Drive:
    Scope Use/Purpose
    https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.readonly Access your file information in read-only mode to navigate your files and play them in Kodi.
    https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.photos.readonly Access your photos in read-only mode to navigate your photos and display them in Kodi.
    profile Access your profile information to display your account as an option in Kodi.
  • This project is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Google, Microsoft, Kodi, OneDrive, Google Drive, Google Photos, or any cloud storage provider.
